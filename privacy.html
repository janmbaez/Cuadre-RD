CuadreRD policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Summary

CuadreRD is designed to process personal finance data locally on your iPhone and use email access only with your authorization.

1. Overview

CuadreRD is a personal finance iOS app for users in the Dominican Republic. It helps users organize bank alert emails, track expenses, detect balances when available, review uncertain transactions, and use Siri shortcuts. This Privacy Policy explains how CuadreRD handles information when you use the app.

2. Email Access and OAuth

CuadreRD may request access to your Gmail account through Google OAuth only after you choose to connect it. The app uses user-authorized email data to identify financial alerts, such as bank transaction notifications, balance notices, deposits, withdrawals, card charges, and similar finance-related messages.

CuadreRD does not request email access to sell data, build advertising profiles, or read unrelated messages for marketing. OAuth access is used to support the app features you choose to enable. You can revoke Google access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

3. iCloud Mail and Other Mail Data

If you use iCloud Mail or another supported mail source, CuadreRD may process user-provided or user-authorized mail data to identify financial alerts. Processing is limited to helping the app recognize and organize finance-related notifications.

4. Information Processed

Depending on the features you use, CuadreRD may process the following categories of information:

  • Email metadata and content needed to identify financial alert messages.
  • Transaction details found in alerts, such as merchant names, dates, amounts, account references, categories, and balances when included by the bank.
  • App data you create or edit, such as review decisions, categories, notes, settings, shortcuts, and preferences.
  • Technical information needed for authentication, such as OAuth tokens, when required to keep your connected account working.

5. Local and On-Device Storage

CuadreRD is designed as a local-first app. Personal finance records are intended to be stored on your device unless you choose to use Apple or system features that sync, back up, or transfer app data. The app does not require a CuadreRD server to sell, broker, or monetize your financial data.

6. How Information Is Used

CuadreRD uses processed information to:

  • Detect and organize bank alert emails.
  • Create and update personal transaction records.
  • Estimate balances when balance information appears in supported alerts.
  • Flag uncertain transactions for the Review Inbox.
  • Support Siri shortcuts and other iOS app features you enable.
  • Improve reliability, security, and user control inside the app.

7. Data Sharing and Sale

CuadreRD does not sell your personal information. CuadreRD does not use your financial alert data for third-party advertising. Information is not shared with banks, advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties for their own marketing purposes.

8. User Control, Sign Out, and Deletion

You control whether to connect mail accounts and can disconnect or sign out from supported accounts in the app when those controls are available. You may delete app data from within the app or by deleting the app from your device, subject to any iOS backups or sync services you have enabled.

For Google accounts, you can also revoke CuadreRD's access from your Google Account security settings. After access is revoked, CuadreRD will no longer be able to access new Gmail data through that OAuth connection unless you authorize it again.

9. Data Accuracy

CuadreRD relies on the content and format of bank alert messages. Detected transactions, categories, balances, taxes, forecasts, or summaries may be incomplete or incorrect. You should review important information directly with your financial institution.

10. Security

CuadreRD uses platform security features available on iOS and follows a local-first approach to reduce unnecessary data exposure. No app can guarantee perfect security, and you are responsible for keeping your device, Apple ID, Google Account, and passcodes secure.

11. Children's Privacy

CuadreRD is intended for personal finance organization and is not directed to children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact the app developer through the support channel listed with the app.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When it changes, the Last updated date above will be revised. Continued use of CuadreRD after an update means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the CuadreRD developer through the support channel provided with the app listing or repository where this website is published.
