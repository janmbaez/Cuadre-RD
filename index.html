Mail sync, explained plainly

CuadreRD uses authorized email access only to find your financial alerts.

If you connect Gmail, CuadreRD requests OAuth permission so the app can read relevant email data and identify messages that look like bank alerts. If you use iCloud Mail, the app can process user-provided or authorized mail data for the same purpose.

The goal is not to replace your bank, move money, or read unrelated messages for advertising. The goal is to help you convert alerts you already receive into a more useful personal finance view.
